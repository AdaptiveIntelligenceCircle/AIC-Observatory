# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The AIC Observatory community is committed to fostering an open, respectful, and collaborative environment.

Participants are expected to contribute to a constructive and ethical research ecosystem.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants should:

* act with respect and professionalism
* support constructive scientific discussion
* welcome diverse perspectives
* prioritize responsible technology development
* avoid harassment, discrimination, or hostility

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* harassment or intimidation
* hate speech or discrimination
* attempts to misuse the project for surveillance or manipulation
* unethical data collection practices
* intentional misinformation

---

## Ethical Research Expectations

Contributors must respect the project’s core principles:

* privacy protection
* responsible data use
* transparency
* harm minimization

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

[maintainers@aic-observatory.org](mailto:maintainers@aic-observatory.org)

All reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or restrict contributions from individuals who violate this code of conduct.

Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the community.
