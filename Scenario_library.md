# Scenario Library

## Overview

The Scenario Library contains a collection of possible future scenarios explored by AIC Observatory.

Scenarios are used to:

* explore potential futures
* stress-test civilization models
* evaluate systemic risks
* analyze potential policy interventions

Scenarios are exploratory rather than predictive.

---

# Scenario Categories

Scenarios are grouped into several broad categories.

```id="scenstruct"
Scenario Library
│
├── Technology Futures
├── Economic Transformation
├── Information Ecosystem Futures
├── Meaning and Social Dynamics
└── Systemic Risk Scenarios
```

---

# Technology Futures

### AI Acceleration Scenario

Rapid breakthroughs in artificial intelligence capabilities accelerate technological change across industries.

Possible consequences:

* automation expansion
* rapid innovation cycles
* new forms of economic organization
* increased AI governance challenges

---

### Compute Oligopoly Scenario

Compute infrastructure becomes concentrated among a small number of organizations.

Possible consequences:

* centralization of technological power
* barriers to open innovation
* geopolitical competition for compute resources

---

### Open Source AI Explosion

Open-source AI ecosystems expand dramatically.

Possible consequences:

* democratized innovation
* faster capability diffusion
* increased safety and governance challenges

---

# Economic Transformation

### Automation Shock

Automation significantly disrupts labor markets across multiple sectors.

Possible consequences:

* economic inequality
* workforce transitions
* new economic models

---

### AI-Driven Productivity Boom

Artificial intelligence dramatically increases productivity and economic output.

Possible consequences:

* new industries
* changes in global economic structures
* shifts in geopolitical power

---

# Information Ecosystem Futures

### AI-Generated Media Flood

AI-generated content becomes dominant in digital information ecosystems.

Possible consequences:

* reduced information trust
* misinformation amplification
* cognitive overload

---

### Algorithmic Attention Fragmentation

Digital platforms increasingly optimize for engagement rather than knowledge quality.

Possible consequences:

* reduced public discourse quality
* attention instability
* societal polarization

---

# Meaning and Social Dynamics

### Meaning Crisis Expansion

Societal meaning structures weaken due to technological disruption and digital environments.

Possible consequences:

* social fragmentation
* identity instability
* increased psychological stress

---

### Human Flourishing Renaissance

Societies successfully adapt to technological change and strengthen human meaning structures.

Possible consequences:

* healthier digital ecosystems
* stronger communities
* new cultural institutions

---

# Systemic Risk Scenarios

### Technological Cascade Failure

Multiple technological systems fail simultaneously due to interdependence.

Possible consequences:

* infrastructure disruptions
* economic instability
* governance crises

---

### AI Governance Fragmentation

Countries develop incompatible AI governance frameworks.

Possible consequences:

* regulatory conflict
* technology fragmentation
* international tension

# Scenario Evolution

The scenario library should continuously evolve as:

* new signals emerge
* models improve
* global conditions change

Scenarios are not static predictions but evolving analytical tools for understanding the future.
